About Us

With cutting-edge machinery and extensive industry expertise, SES proudly stands out as one of Africa's best. We specialize in producing heavy-duty machinery parts for the mining, construction, timber, and agricultural industries, alongside expert welding and metal fabrication services. Additionally, we are committed to training artisans and have constructed a cupola furnace to recycle unused scrap metals efficiently.

At SES, our vision is to become the leading local authority in welding and metal fabrication. We prioritize uncompromising quality in our products and services, ensuring excellence in every aspect of our work.

We foster a respectful, safe, and conducive environment for our team, firmly believing that success is built through collaboration. At SES, we live by the principle that "Together Everyone Achieves More (TEAM)."

OUR SERVICES

AGRICULTURAL WELDING

We provide welding services tailored to the agricultural sector, repairing and fabricating equipment to keep your farm operations running smoothly. Our solutions are durable and designed to withstand tough conditions.

STRUCTURAL WELDING

Our structural welding services focus on creating strong and reliable frameworks for buildings and infrastructure. We ensure precision and adherence to safety standards in every project.

TRAINING SERVICES

Enhance your skills with our professional welding training programs. Our experienced instructors provide hands-on learning to prepare you for industry demands.

REPAIR & MAINTENANCE

Keep your equipment and structures in top condition with our reliable welding repairs and maintenance services. We work to extend the lifespan and functionality of your assets.

PIPE WELDING

We specialize in welding pipelines with precision to ensure strong, leak-proof joints. From installation to repair, we deliver dependable solutions for all your pipe welding needs.

METAL FABRICATION

Our metal fabrication services offer custom solutions for high-quality parts and products. We combine expertise and advanced techniques to meet your unique needs.
